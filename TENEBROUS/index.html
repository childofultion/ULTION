09 Dec 2025 – a shift underway

i’ve been watching more closely these days. not just movies — the world, the silence, the colours that drift through each hour. cinema has become a place where i can sit with myself without interruption. a place that asks for patience, intention, and presence. i’m learning to follow that. two films every week. not as a checklist, but as a way to sharpen how i see. some of them pull me into stranger worlds. some take me apart quietly. some leave a feeling i can’t name until days later. i’m starting to understand that’s the point — not clarity, but recognition. somewhere in all of this, i’ve noticed my faith sitting beside me in the quiet — not loud, not structured, just… there. the same way it shows up in moments when i’m making music, or when the world slows down long enough for me to finally listen. not religion. not routine. just that inner voice that’s been with me longer than i admit. the music shifts with the seasons of this journey. dreamy textures when i need softness. sparse piano when the films grow heavy. left-field rhythms when the stories tilt toward the surreal. sound has become a companion to the images — the bridge between what i watch and what i carry with me, and sometimes it feels like the closest thing to prayer i have. i’m not trying to escape. i’m trying to expand. to let these films teach me how to look again — slower, deeper, with more honesty. to let my faith grow in the same way — quietly, without performance, without forcing myself into anyone else’s shape. as the world moves at its own pace, i’m choosing a different one. one shaped by art that asks something of me. one shaped by the discipline of paying attention. one shaped by a belief that still lives in me, even when i don’t dress it up or name it out loud. there’s beauty in committing to that. and faith in what it might reveal. more soon.
